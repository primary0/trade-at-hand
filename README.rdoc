== Maldives Trade-at-Hand Project

Trade-at-Hand is a project initiated by the Internal Trade Center with the objective to make innovative use of mobile phones by business exporters from developing economies. The service started to serve agricultural product exporters and now moves on to other economic sectors.

Trade-at-Hand was launched in Maldives under the name of mvLeads. The project was cordinated by Raphael Dard (ITC Trade-at-Hand Projects) with the Ministry of Trade and Economic Development. The application was developed by Kubenn Private Limited.

Maldives Trade-at-Hand project has been discontinued until further notice.
